excerpt: 'With [Courtney Fung](https://www.courtneyfung.com/). How do emerging powers secure international leadership posts, and how do these international civil-service positions contribute to influence in multilateral institutions? Focusing on China in the UN system, we find that China forms coalitions with weaker states to control leadership appointments&mdash;states with higher UN General Assembly voting affinity with China secure greater increases in UN leadership positions&mdash;and that China-friendly leadership correlates with the frequency of PRC-specific and PRC-reinterpreted terms across 54 UN departments&rsquo; documentation.'
